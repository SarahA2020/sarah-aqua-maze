Aqua Maze

You have been trapped in an underwater world! Navigate your way through the maze and make it to the escape submarine before your time runs out.

Be sure not to stray from the maze path or run into the hungry deep-sea creatures - they will be only too happy to gobble you up!

Place your cursor on the starfish to get ready to play. Once your cursor leaves the starfish and enters the maze the timer will begin counting down.

Good luck!
